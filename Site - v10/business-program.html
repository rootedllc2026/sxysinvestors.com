Financial Commitment Statement

By submitting this program listing with a paid or stipend compensation type, you acknowledge and agree that:

1. You commit to compensating the apprentice(s) at the agreed rate for the full duration of the program unless the program is formally ended in accordance with the terms agreed upon at placement.

2. Failure to honor this financial commitment may result in removal of your program listing and ineligibility from future participation in SXY's Corporate Club Apprenticeship Program.

3. SXY's Corporate Club serves as a community connector — compensation arrangements are entered into directly between you (the business) and the apprentice. SXY's Corporate Club is not a party to any employment agreement.
