How It Works
Simple. Structured. Supported.

Our strategists review every application and business program to make sure the match is meaningful — not just a placement.

01
📋

Submit Your Application

Members apply through our guided chat with Sasha, our AI assistant. Businesses complete a program submission form. Our strategists review all submissions.

02
🔍

Program Review

Our team evaluates each business program for structure, compensation commitment, and fit. Qualified programs are posted to our board for applicants to review.

03
📬

Applications Reviewed

At the close of applications, our team reviews all candidates, notes any relevant context, and forwards qualified applicants to the business owner — who makes the final hiring decision.

04
🎉

Placement & Announcement

Winners are notified via email. With their permission, placements are celebrated in the SXY's Corporate Club community hub.

Member Eligibility
Who Can Apply?

The apprenticeship program is reserved for our most committed VIP members. Here's what you need to qualify.

👑
Active VIP Membership

You must hold an active VIP membership at SXY's Corporate Club to be eligible to apply.

📅
6 Months of Membership

Applicants must have been VIP members in good standing for a minimum of six consecutive months.

📄
Supporting Materials

A resume and any relevant portfolio or certifications are required as part of your application.

🤝
Employer Interview

Shortlisted candidates will complete an interview directly with the business owner or hiring contact.
