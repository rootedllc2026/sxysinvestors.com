Common Questions
Before You Apply
What exactly do I get each month for $300? +
It depends on which retainer you choose. Account Management delivers client systems, CRM work, communication cadences, and a monthly success report. Strategic Programming delivers two 60-minute sessions, a 90-day action plan, SOP documentation, tool setup, and implementation support. Both retainers produce tangible, usable outputs — not just advice.
Is there a contract? Can I really cancel anytime? +
No long-term contracts. These retainers are month-to-month. We ask for 30 days notice to wrap up any open deliverables cleanly, but there's no penalty or cancellation fee.
What's the difference between the two retainers? Can I get both? +
Account Management focuses outward — on your clients and their experience with you after the sale. Strategic Programming focuses inward — on how your business operates, plans, and grows. They complement each other and many clients run both simultaneously for $600/month total.
Do I need to have a lot of clients for Account Management to make sense? +
Not at all. If you have as few as 5–10 paying clients and no formal system to manage their experience, you're already losing retention and referral revenue. We build the infrastructure now so it scales as you grow.
I'm not very tech-savvy. Will I be able to use what you build? +
Yes — that's actually the point. We select tools that fit your technical comfort level and document everything with clear SOPs and walkthroughs so your team can use and maintain what we build long after the engagement.
What cities or regions do you serve? +
We're Philadelphia-based and work with clients across Pennsylvania and New Jersey — including West Chester, Lancaster, Pittsburgh, Scranton, King of Prussia, Cherry Hill, Princeton, Hoboken, and Newark. All engagements are virtual, so we also work with clients nationwide.
